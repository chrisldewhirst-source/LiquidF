## ðŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

### "Calculator isn't working"
- Check JavaScript is enabled in browser
- Try clearing cache and refresh
- Test in different browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox)

### "Email links aren't working"
- Update `mailto:` links to your actual email
- Format: `mailto:youremail@domain.com?subject=Discovery%20Call`

### "Not getting any traffic"
- Website alone won't generate leads
- You must actively promote (LinkedIn, email, ads)
- Focus on quality over quantity
- Target specific industries where you have case studies

### "Discovery calls not converting"
- Are you following the X-Y-Z framework?
- Are you showing ROI calculations?
- Are you closing with Blueprint Workshop proposal?
- Record calls and review for improvement
